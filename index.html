About

Welcome to my portfolio! My name is Léo Le Gall, and here I showcase my research. I am from Bretagne, France, and I am currently pursuing a PhD in Rostock, Germany. I am passionate about marine biology, fisheries science, and statistics.

My main career challenge is exploring how far mathematics can help us understand marine ecosystems and population dynamics. I am particularly interested in combining quantitative methods with ecological knowledge to address complex questions about the ocean and its inhabitants.
